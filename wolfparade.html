If you're looking at this ~secret page~, that means you're either a huge fan of Wolf Parade (like me) or maybe...possibly...a member of the band?! I should be so lucky!

In ritual preparation for my second and third time seeing Wolf Parade play live since they toured with At Mount Zoomer, I felt that I wanted to give something back to the band that has given me so much. Not only some wonderful music, but also the ability for a band to get back together after so long to keep giving their fans amazing shows.






In case anyone wants to know, my favourite songs are (in no particular order):

  • Kissing the Beehive (At Mount Zoomer)
  • In The Direction Of The Moon (Expo 86)
  • Modern World (Apologies to the Queen Mary)
  • Ghost Pressure (Expo 86)
  • National People's Scare (EP 2004)

I seriously cannot wait to see the band on Friday and Saturday at Lee's Palace. I even still have the shirt I bought at their concert at Koolhaus in 2008. It's gonna be awesome.
